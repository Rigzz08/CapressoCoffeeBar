About Us

What Makes Our Coffee Special?

At Capresso Coffee Bar and Cuisine, we take pride in roasting our premium coffee beans daily to ensure every cup captures the rich, authentic flavors of Jamaica. Our chefs craft exclusive daily specials, highlighting the vibrant tastes of Jamaican cuisine.

Beyond our exceptional food and coffee, we host a variety of special events, from live music nights and chess tournaments to cultural celebrations.

There's always something exciting happening. Check our calendar for upcoming events and make your visit unforgettable.
